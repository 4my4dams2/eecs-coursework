American Literature

The purpose of this website is to provide a resource for lovers of American literature.
It includes American literature across all categories, including short stories, poetry, novels, etc.
American literature is rich in cultural heritage.
However, that heritage is diminishing with the rapid advancement of online, fast-food reading.
Therefore, this website seeks to provide a haven for American literature, and a platform for literary discussion.
Enjoy!
